Zany Plant Brand Guide

Our mission

Plant It!

The company Zany Plant teaches people how to grow their plants successfully. We sell plants that grow well in your zone so that they will have the best chance at thriving. We have an app where you can catalog your plants and set reminders for when they should be planted. We have chats available for any questions on how to care for the plants. Our mission is to get people outdoors and to learn more about plants.

The theme of Zany Plant is success, nature, and knowledge.
